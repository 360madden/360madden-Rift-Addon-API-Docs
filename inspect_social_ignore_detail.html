Inspect.Social.Ignore.Detail

Provides detailed information about an ignored player.

Usage:

detail = Inspect.Social.Ignore.Detail(ignore)
details = Inspect.Social.Ignore.Detail(ignores)
ParameterTypeDatatypeDescription
